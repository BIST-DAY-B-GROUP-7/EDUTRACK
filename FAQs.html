Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduTrack?

EduTrack is an innovative educational platform designed to streamline and enhance the learning experience for both students and teachers.

How do I register?

You can register by clicking on the 'Register' link in the navigation bar and filling out the registration form.

How can I access my course materials?

All your course materials can be found in the 'Course Materials' section of the Student Portal.

Where can I find more help?

You can find more help by visiting our Contact and Support page.
